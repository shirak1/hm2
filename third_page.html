SKILLS

With a diverse and dynamic background, I embarked on my academic journey in 2021, pursuing a degree in Industrial Engineering and Management. Prior to my studies, I dedicated two years of service in the army as a medic, where I honed my skills in crisis management and developed a strong sense of responsibility. Following my military service, I embarked on a six-month adventure in South America, gaining valuable life experiences and broadening my perspectives. Intrigued by my passion for order, organization, and process optimization, I decided to further my education in industrial engineering. My interests lie in the intricate art of streamlining and organizing processes, coupled with effective management and collaboration with diverse teams. This unique combination of experiences has equipped me with a versatile skill set and a deep appreciation for efficiency and teamwork in both professional and personal realms.
